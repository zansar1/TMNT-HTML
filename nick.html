Timmy Turner

Timmy Turner has Fairy Godparents who grant his every wish? Lucky! Well… not quite. Timmy still has to deal with his not-so-smart parents and an evil babysitter named Vicky. Not to mention he’s sharing Cosmo and Wanda with his new neighbor Chloe Carmichael. But Timmy has a deep appreciation for his godparents... who else can get him out of the trouble he always finds himself in?

Cosmo

Ok, so Comso isn’t the smartest fairy in Fairy World, but he is definitely the funniest! Being Timmy’s Fairy Godfather can be hard, but Cosmo keeps the wishes (and the laughs) coming a mile a minute. Plus he’s got a super smart wife to keep him in line. What he lacks in skill, smarts and ability to do things well, he certainly makes up for in enthusiasm. WHEEEEE!

Wanda

Wanda’s a bit on the wacky side, but when it comes to magic, she’s definitely the more able of the two Godparents! When Cosmo and Timmy are totally lost, Wanda is always there to lead them in the “right” direction. She takes her duties very seriously, which means keeping an eye on Timmy and making sure he’s happy… while also keeping her other eye on Cosmo.

Vicky

Vicky is the sixteen-year-old neighborhood girl who babysits the local kids. Even though parents think she’s sweet and trustworthy, she’s actually the biggest bully on the block. Once they’re gone, she turns into Icky Vicky, the most evil babysitter there ever was. The worst part of all? Timmy is her favorite victim.

Mr. and Mrs. Turner

The Turners are happy. All the time. They’re crazy about each other and their son. Life is perfect: they have great jobs, a great house, great son, great everything. Well, except their neighbor, Dinkleberg. He’s not so great. They do everything together and never get tired of each other. So what does all this happiness amount to? A complete lack of awareness that they’ve hired an evil babysitter to take care of their son…who, by the way, has Fairy Godparents.

AJ

A.J. is a super genius who sticks by Timmy’s side through all his problems. He’s got the biggest brain in school and can usually outsmart bullies like Vicky. He might not be a social butterfly, but who needs popularity when your best friend has Fairy Godparents, right?

Chester

Chester’s wild schemes sometimes land Timmy in hot water… but he’s just trying to have fun! This super suave scammer can talk his way out of anything and always has a plan. He may not have a lot of money, or self control around food, but Chester knows how to be a good friend till the end!

Jorgen Von Strangle

Jorgen is the head drill instructor at The Fairy Academy and Cosmo and Wanda’s boss. His mission in life is to instill discipline, respect, and above all else, “fairy pride” in his recruits. Jorgen hates the term “fairy” and prefers to be called a “Magic Marine.”

About The Fairly OddParents

Timmy Turner is an average kid - sort of. He's an only child with oblivious parents. But luckily he has Cosmo and Wanda, his wacky fairy godparents who help him hide from evil babysitters, do homework and much more craziness!
